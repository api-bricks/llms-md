TRC-20 Standard
---------------

This fosters the growth of decentralized finance (**DeFi**) and a vibrant token economy.

Practical Applications of TRC-20 Tokens
---------------------------------------

**TRC-20** tokens are versatile assets used in various applications within the Tron ecosystem. These tokens facilitate easy trading, transferring, and storage on the Tron network.

This makes them integral to decentralized finance (**DeFi**) applications. Additionally, TRC-20 tokens are compatible with a wide range of Tron wallets and exchanges. This enhances their utility and accessibility for users and developers. The versatility of TRC-20 tokens drives their adoption in diverse industries and expands the functional scope of the Tron blockchain.
